About

Bin Butler is a local St. Louis–based service built to make your life easier. We’re here to take the hassle out of trash day by managing your bins for you—reliably, every week.

What started as a simple idea—helping neighbors save time—has grown into a trusted, community-driven service. We believe small conveniences make a big difference, and our goal is to bring that convenience right to your driveway.

Sit back, relax, and let Bin Butler handle your bins so you don’t have to!

What & Cost

Tired of dragging your bins to the curb every week? Bin Butler takes care of it for you! We handle your trash, recycling, and yard waste bins—bringing them to the curb the night before pickup and returning them neatly once they’re emptied.

Our service is just $50 per month—no hidden fees, no contracts, just convenience. Whether you’re busy, traveling, or simply want one less thing to worry about, Bin Butler makes trash day effortless.

Rules

1.Please make sure your garage door is open on pickup day if your bin is kept in the garage so we can access it. If it’s closed, we won’t be able to complete the service.

2. If your bin is behind a gate, kindly leave it unlocked or provide a code so we can get through. Locked or tricky gates might mean we have to skip the pickup.

3.For everyone’s safety, please keep dogs and other pets secured during service. If pets are outside or not contained, we’ll need to skip the service.

4.Keep your bin’s lid closed with no more than 12 inches open. Overloaded bins may be skipped for pickup.

5. Keep the area clear of obstacles.

6.We do not carry heavy items—only normal residential bins.
